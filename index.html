Tiny Melody – Support

Help, guides, and contact for the Tiny Melody app

1. About the App

Tiny Melody turns your iPhone into a warm, playable music box. Tap the keys, record a melody, edit the notes, and share it as a tiny musical gift.

  • 15 touch-sensitive music box keys with per-note colors
  • Record and save unlimited melodies (up to 3 in free version)
  • Three sound presets: Classic, Wooden, Dream
  • Smart timing smoothing (Original / Smooth mode)
  • Simple note editor with undo/redo
  • Audio and video export with system share sheet
  • 6 built-in classic melodies included
  • Core Haptics feedback for key taps
  • One-time purchase for full unlock

2. Getting Started

  1. Open the app — you'll see 15 golden keys on the Play screen.
  2. Tap any key to hear the music box sound. Each key has a unique color and note letter.
  3. Slide your finger across multiple keys to play a melody.
  4. Tap Record — a 3-2-1 countdown appears. Play your melody.
  5. Tap Stop — preview your recording. Switch between Original and Smooth timing.
  6. Name your melody and tap Save. It appears in My Melodies.
  7. Tap a saved melody to play, edit, or share it.

3. Features Guide

🎹 Playing

Keys are arranged from low (left, longer) to high (right, shorter). Use the octave switcher above the keys to shift the range. You can play with up to 10 fingers simultaneously.

⏺ Recording

Tap Record to start. The countdown gives you 3 seconds to prepare. Recording stops automatically at 3 minutes. If you play no notes, the recording is discarded. You can disable the countdown in Settings.

✨ Smooth Timing

Smooth gently adjusts your note timing toward a detected beat grid, without changing pitch or note order. The strength is 45% — your original feel is preserved, just slightly tidier. The Original version is always kept.

🎨 Sound Presets

Classic — Bright and timeless. Default sound with clear high harmonics and moderate decay (~2.2s).
Wooden — Soft, warm, and gentle. Muted highs, stronger body resonance, shorter decay (~1.5s).
Dream — Airy and slow to fade. Rich reverb, wide stereo, longer decay (~3.0s).

Classic is free. Wooden and Dream are unlocked with the full version.

✏️ Editing

In the editor, you can drag notes to change their pitch or timing, tap empty space to add a note, select and delete notes, and adjust overall tempo with a slider. Undo/redo is supported for up to 50 actions.

4. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need an internet connection?
A: No. All features work offline. No account or login required.
Q: How many melodies can I save in the free version?
A: The free version allows up to 3 saved melodies. The full version has unlimited storage.
Q: How do I unlock the full version?
A: Tap "Unlock Tiny Melody" in Settings or the app will prompt you when you reach the free limit (4th melody, or selecting Wooden/Dream sound). It's a one-time purchase through the App Store.
Q: Can I transfer my melodies to a new device?
A: Currently, melodies are stored locally and do not sync via iCloud. A future update may add cloud sync. Your purchase can be restored on any device using the same Apple ID.
Q: Why is there no sound when my iPhone is on silent?
A: Tiny Melody uses the playback audio category, which plays through the speaker even in silent mode. If you still hear no sound, check that your volume isn't turned all the way down and that Bluetooth isn't connected to a silent output.
Q: The keys sound the same / no pitch difference?
A: This usually indicates the audio engine hasn't initialized properly. Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, check that your device isn't in Low Power Mode, which can affect audio performance.
Q: How do I delete a melody?
A: In My Melodies, swipe left on a melody and tap Delete. You'll be asked to confirm.
Q: Can I export my melodies as MIDI?
A: Not yet. MIDI import/export is planned for a future update.
Q: How is my data protected?
A: All data stays on your device. Tiny Melody has no servers, no tracking, and no analytics. See our Privacy Policy for details.

5. Troubleshooting

  • No sound: Check volume level, ensure your device isn't connected to Bluetooth headphones, and restart the app.
  • App crashes on launch: Make sure your device is running iOS 17 or later. Try reinstalling the app.
  • Recording won't start: If you just finished a recording, wait a moment or navigate away and back to the Play tab to reset the recording state.
  • Purchase not restoring: Tap "Restore Purchase" in Settings. Make sure you're signed in to the correct Apple ID.
